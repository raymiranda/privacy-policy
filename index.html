Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Overview

This service ("Jarvis SMS") is a personal messaging assistant operated by Raymond Mira for private, non-commercial use. It sends SMS messages on behalf of the account owner only.

Information We Collect

This service processes phone numbers and message content solely for the purpose of delivering SMS messages as directed by the account owner. No data is collected from message recipients.

How We Use Information

Phone numbers and message content are used exclusively to send and deliver SMS messages. Messages are not stored beyond what is necessary for delivery confirmation.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal information with third parties. Message delivery is facilitated through Twilio's messaging platform, subject to Twilio's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Message logs may be retained for up to 30 days for troubleshooting purposes, after which they are deleted.

Opt-Out

Any recipient may reply STOP to any message to opt out of future communications. Opt-out requests are honored immediately.

Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Changes take effect immediately upon posting.
